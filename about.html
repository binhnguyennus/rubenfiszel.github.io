About me

Hello! I’m Ruben, a Master student of Computer Science with a Minor in Financial Engineering at EPFL in Lausanne, Switzerland.

I am passionate about Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. I am also a Scala and Functionnal Programming enthusiast.

I believe in the symbiose between practice and theory: I strive to be able to build things by myself, while not being oblivious to the math behind it.

I believe in an AI revolution and if you catch me not coding, I like to talk to about a variety of subjects, especially entrepreneurship, finance, economy, politics and philosophy.

For a semester project, I wrote scala-drl and I co-authored a paper (in french) about the transformation of the society by AI.

I did a summer internship at Skymind, the deeplearning4j company. I wrote their reinforcement learning framework (including DQN and A3C), rl4.

During the 2017 spring semester, I will be working at the Stanford PPL lab on Delite for my master project that will finalize my master.
